The Beatles

About The Beatles: The Beatles, the legendary British band formed in Liverpool in 1960, revolutionized popular music with their innovative sound and songwriting. Comprising John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr, they crafted timeless hits like "Hey Jude," "Let It Be," and "A Hard Day's Night." Their fusion of rock, pop, and experimental elements reshaped music's landscape and inspired countless artists. Known for their charismatic personalities and cultural impact, The Beatles' influence extends beyond music into fashion, film, and social change, making them one of the most iconic and enduring musical acts in history.
